My Hobby:
Playing the Guitar

Photo gallery of Guitars

🎶 Introduction 🎶

One of my favourite hobby is to play the guitar.
This is because playing the guitar helps me to relax and wind down from the stresses of life.
On top of that, playing the guitar helps me to truly immerse myself in a musical world.


🎼How did I learn the guitar?🎼

I was very blessed with the opportunity to learn guitar when I was in secondary 2 under my school's music curriculum.
The teacher who taught me was really passionate with teaching us and this actually started my interest in guitar.
For someone with no prior musical knowledge, he was able to scaffold the learning to one where it was really easy to follow.
He also taught us various ways and methods to learn chords.
He started with teaching us the more common chords first such as the Em, Dm, G and C chords as they were easier to master, before we proceeded to learn more difficult chords such as the F and D7 chords.

😊 My Guitar journey 😊

After learning it in Secondary 2, I was truly amazed and in love with this musical instrument and really wanted to learn more about it.
As a result, I decided to take it up as my Co-Curriculum Activity in Junior College.
Being a part of the Guitar Ensemble team really further enhanced my love and understanding for this instrument.
I was able to have the opportunity to learn a different way of playing the instrument, by learning how to play tabs.
I also learned how to play together as an ensemble and this was really an enriching and meaningful experience as it taught me that team work truly makes the dream works!

The brown one (guitar on the left) is a classical guitar which I have saved up by working part-time jobs to buy it when I was in my junior college days!
